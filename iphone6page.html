Description

Bigger and better

iPhone 6 has been redesigned to not only be bigger, but also better in every way. Although larger, it's dramatically thinner.

With increased power and even better efficiency, the smooth metal surface seamlessly blends into the new Retina HD display, giving you a device that's a perfect out-of-this-world blend of hardware and software.

Hugely powerful. Enormously efficient.

The iPhone 6 Processor, the new A8 chip is built on 64-bit desktop architecture, delivering more power while driving a larger display. The M8 motion co-processor efficiently gathers data from advanced sensors and a new barometer. The iPhone 6 also has increased battery life, letting you do so much more, for longer than ever.

The camera that changed photos now does the same for video. More people take more photos with iPhone than with any other camera.

The iSight camera has a new sensor with Focus Pixels and new video features, like 1080p HD at 60 fps, slo-mo at 240 fps, and time-lapse video mode.

So you'll have more reasons to capture more moments on video, too. All-new sensor supports Focus Pixels and enables even faster autofocus.

The biggest iOS release ever.

iOS 8 is the worlds most advanced mobile operating system. And with incredible new capabilities and features designed to make the most of a larger display, iOS 8 doesn't just work seamlessly with iPhone 6. It makes every experience feel bigger and better.
