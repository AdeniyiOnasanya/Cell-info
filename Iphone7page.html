Description

Yes, this is 7! Underscoring a sleek, tough, and stylish exterior and every bit as powerful as it looks, the iPhone 7 is a user's delight. A dramatic improvement of the most important aspects of the iPhone experience, it introduces advanced new camera systems and provides the best performance and battery life ever in an iPhone. Immersive stereo speakers that give life to your playlist and the brightest and most colourful display, it is equally splash and water resistant. It comes in an array of 5 oustanding colours (Black, Jet Black, Rose Gold, Gold & Silver) and 3 memory capacities (32GB, 64GB & 128GB).

King Of All Phone Displays

Your day deserves a bit more brightness and Apple responded with the iPhone 7 that brings everything to life in colourful details with its amazing display. Photos, messages, news, and countless other things that you interact with take on a life of their own as the iPhone 7 display uses the same color space as the digital cinema industry, so what you see will be noticeably more brilliant and vibrant. With 3D Touch, your phone responds in even more subtle ways, you don't just press, you feel.

The Lightweight Champion

Add a slim, sleek design to amazing size. The iPhone 7 is light as a feather at just 138 grams, it defies all your expectations. You have to feel it to understand.

Wifi and Worldwide Roaming Just Got Better

With advanced LTE that provides with speeds up to 450 Mbps for data downloads — over 50 percent faster than iPhone 6s and three times faster than iPhone 6.6, the iPhone 7 makes travelling easier. You even have more LTE bands, which guarantees the best worldwide roaming available in a smartphone.
